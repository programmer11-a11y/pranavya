Discovery & Pre-Migration Audit

We do a full inventory of your WordPress site, including posts, pages, taxonomies, media, plugins and top performing URLs. Exporting your WordPress content is a key part of this audit and inventory process, so we can review and analyze all assets. We export current sitemaps and crawl the site to find traffic driving pages and backlinks, which become the basis of the master redirect map.

Backup & Staging Setup

Full backup of your WordPress files and database is taken and a staging environment is set up on your server or ours, with the correct hosting environment configured to ensure performance and security during migration. All migration activities happen in staging so your live site is untouched.

Content Export from WordPress

We export WordPress content via XML or CSV and collect all media files. For complex content structures or custom post types we create a detailed field-mapping plan to ensure no data is lost during migration.

Setting Up October CMS

Getting started with October CMS is designed to be easy even for teams moving from WordPress. The setup process involves installing the platform, configuring your database and setting up the backend environment. October CMS has an intuitive interface for managing content so you can create and edit pages, posts and custom content types.

The platform has a robust plugin and theme ecosystem so you can quickly add functionality and tailor the CMS to your needs. Whether you’re creating new pages, managing posts or implementing advanced content types October CMS has the tools to streamline content management and make your site flexible and scalable. With a few simple steps—database creation, platform installation and backend configuration—you’ll be ready to start building and managing your new site.

Import into October CMS

We use October’s community import tools and import behaviors to map XML/CSV data into pages, blog posts or Tailor blueprints. Proven import plugins and custom scripts preserve metadata, dates and author information. For complex mappings we use October’s Import/Export behavior or custom import classes.

Media Migration & URL Rewriting

All images and attachments are copied to October’s storage system. We automate URL rewriting and update database paths so media loads correctly from new locations, no broken media links.

Theme & Functionality

Rebuild Your WordPress theme templates are recreated in October CMS using Twig and Tailor where applicable. WordPress plugin functionality is reimplemented with October plugins, Tailor fields or lightweight custom code for forms, comments and widgets.

October CMS has a visual editor so content teams can edit and preview content during and after migration.

SEO Preservation: Redirects, Metadata & Sitemaps

We implement the redirect map as 301 redirects (either server-level or via plugins), preserve meta titles and descriptions and regenerate XML sitemaps for submission to Google Search Console. 301 redirects are crucial to preserve link equity during migration.

QA, Performance & Accessibility Checks

Full pre-launch crawl to verify internal links, structured data and hreflang tags if applicable. Migrating to October CMS prevents performance slowdowns often experienced with traditional CMS platforms as content and traffic grows. We test site speed on mobile and desktop and fix any 4xx/5xx errors. Structured data and canonical tags are validated for SEO compliance.

Launch & Monitoring

We perform the DNS switch or point the site live, re-run crawls and monitor Google Search Console and analytics for any ranking or indexing changes. We’re on-call for the first 2-4 weeks post-launch to fix any issues.

October CMS is scalable so your site will perform well as traffic grows.

Customization and Integration

October CMS is designed to be customized and integrated with many other platforms and tools. Through its API support and rich library of plugins you can connect your CMS to CRM systems, ERP solutions, payment gateways and social media platforms so your website works seamlessly within your broader digital ecosystem.

The platform has custom fields and meta tags so you can create unique content types and structures to fit your business needs. Using the command line interface developers can install plugins, manage themes and configure advanced settings making October CMS a powerful choice for projects that require deep customization and integration with other platforms. This flexibility means your CMS can evolve with your business and support new features and integrations as your needs change.

Omnichannel Content Delivery

One of the biggest advantages of using a headless CMS like October CMS is the ability to deliver content across multiple platforms and devices, supporting true omnichannel content delivery. With robust APIs and content management tools October CMS allows you to deliver content not only to your website but also to mobile apps, smart devices and other digital touchpoints.

The platform supports multiple content distribution channels: social media, email newsletters and RSS feeds so you can reach your audience wherever they are. By centralizing content management and using multiple platforms for distribution October CMS helps you create a consistent user experience across all channels. This means your team can manage content efficiently and your brand message stays unified no matter how or where your users interact with your content.
